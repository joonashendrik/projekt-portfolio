Honors

Mickey Mouse has received ten nominations for the Academy Award for best animated short film;

four of Mickey's cartoons were included in the book The 50 Greatest Cartoons;

On November 18, 1978, in honor of his 50th anniversary, Mickey became the first cartoon character to have a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame;

Mickey Mouse was appointed at Moomba festival as their King of Moomba (1977).

Mickey received the honor as Grand Marshal of the Tournament of Roses Parade on New Year's Day 2005 as first cartoon character.
